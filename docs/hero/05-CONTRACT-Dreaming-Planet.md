## Purpose

Dreaming Planets exist to welcome Non-Storyteller Explorers into
VihuPlanet.

A Dreaming Planet is not owned by anyone.

It is waiting for the next Storyteller.

## Contract

### Availability

The visible Hero Page universe always contains at least one Dreaming
Planet.

There may be more than one Dreaming Planet.

The exact number is an implementation decision.

### Awakening

When an Explorer clicks a Dreaming Planet, the Dreaming Planet awakens.

Its Companion also awakens.

The Companion interacts with the Explorer.

Multiple outcomes are possible.

### Explorer Path

The Explorer may simply continue exploring.

The Companion never pressures the Explorer.

The Dreaming Planet gently returns to sleep.

Nothing is lost.

The Companion patiently waits.

### Storyteller Path

If the Explorer chooses to begin their journey, the Companion welcomes
them.

The Dreaming Planet becomes a Young Story World.

The Young Story World will grow together with the Storyteller throughout
their creative journey.

### Existing Storyteller Path

If an existing Storyteller awakens a Dreaming Planet, the Companion
gently reminds them that they already belong to VihuPlanet.

The Dreaming Planet does not become another Story World.

The Storyteller is lovingly guided back toward their own Story World.

### Product Philosophy

A Dreaming Planet represents possibility.

It never pressures.

It never expires.

It always waits with patience.
