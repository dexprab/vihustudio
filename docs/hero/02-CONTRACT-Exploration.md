## Purpose

The Hero Page exists to invite exploration.

Exploration is the first experience of every visitor.

No one enters VihuPlanet as a customer.

No one enters as a user.

Everyone enters as an Explorer.

## Contract

### Explorer Hierarchy

```
Explorer
├── Non-Storyteller Explorer
└── Storyteller Explorer
```

Explorer is the highest identity inside VihuPlanet.

A Storyteller never stops being an Explorer.

Storyteller is simply a deeper relationship with VihuPlanet.

### Non-Storyteller Explorer

A Non-Storyteller Explorer may:

- Explore VihuPlanet.
- Visit Story Worlds.
- Discover stories.
- Read publicly shared creations.
- Experience the world.

A Non-Storyteller Explorer does not yet have:

- Their own Story World.
- Their own Companion.
- Their own creations stored inside VihuPlanet.

### Storyteller Explorer

A Storyteller Explorer continues exploring while also belonging to
VihuPlanet.

They have:

- Their own Story World.
- Their own Companion.
- Their own creative works.
- Their own growing place inside VihuPlanet.

### Becoming a Storyteller

A Non-Storyteller Explorer does not become a Storyteller through
registration.

Instead, they become a Storyteller through one of the magical
transformation experiences defined by the Hero Page.

Examples include:

- Awakening the Dreaming Planet.
- Awakening a Dreaming Story World.

Registration is an implementation detail.

Becoming a Storyteller is the emotional experience.

### Exploration Principle

Exploration is always free.

Wonder should never require authentication.

### Interaction Principle

Children learn through touching, clicking and curiosity.

Every meaningful object on the Hero Page should respond to interaction.

The world teaches through exploration rather than instruction.

### Storyteller Eligibility

Anyone may become an Explorer.

VihuPlanet is designed primarily for children between the ages of 5 and
15.

Explorer has no age restriction.

Storyteller follows a soft age guideline intended to preserve the
audience VihuPlanet is designed for.

The Hero Page should never make a child feel rejected because of age.

How eligibility is implemented is an engineering decision and not part
of this contract.
