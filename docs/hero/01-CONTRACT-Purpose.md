## Purpose

The Hero Page is the first page every child sees before entering VihuPlanet.

It is not VihuPlanet itself.

It is the magical threshold between the real world and the child's
creative world.

The Hero Page prepares the child emotionally before they enter.

### What is VihuPlanet?

VihuPlanet is the permanent home of a child's creativity.

It is where all of their creative work lives.

Examples include:

- Stories
- Books
- Drawings
- Comics
- Journals
- Ideas
- Future creative projects

### Core Promise

VihuPlanet never replaces a child's creativity.

It never changes the child's imagination.

It never creates over what is original.

Instead, VihuPlanet reveals and beautifies the creativity that already
exists.

We preserve what is real. We beautify what already exists.

### Product Philosophy

The child is always the creator.

AI is never the creator.

AI exists only to help the child's imagination shine.
