## Purpose

The Hero Page is not a traditional homepage.

It is a living Sky Map.

Every Explorer exists at one position inside the VihuPlanet universe.

The visible Hero Page represents only the nearby portion of the universe
surrounding that Explorer.

## Contract

### Visible Universe

The Hero Page contains nearby Story Worlds.

Dreaming Planets.

The Explorer's own Story World (if they are a Storyteller).

Other nearby worlds.

### Infinite Universe

Beyond the visible universe exist countless Story Worlds.

These appear only as distant lights.

They are not immediately visitable.

### Telescope

The Telescope is the only way to look beyond the visible universe.

Through the Telescope, distant Story Worlds can be observed and visited.
