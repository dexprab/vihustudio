## Purpose

A Storyteller is an Explorer who has been welcomed into VihuPlanet.

Becoming a Storyteller is not creating an account.

It is the beginning of a lifelong relationship with VihuPlanet.

## Contract

### Becoming a Storyteller

A Non-Storyteller Explorer becomes a Storyteller through one of the
magical transformation experiences defined by the Hero Page.

This transformation marks the beginning of three lifelong relationships.

### Relationship 01 — Companion

Every Storyteller receives a Companion.

The Companion is more than a guide.

The Companion:

- explores alongside the Storyteller
- helps them discover VihuPlanet
- helps them navigate the universe
- encourages creativity
- celebrates achievements
- grows together with the Storyteller

The Companion is a lifelong friend.

It is never a tutorial.

It is never simply an assistant.

### Relationship 02 — Story World

Every Storyteller receives their own Story World.

The Story World is their home inside VihuPlanet.

It grows together with the Storyteller.

It changes through:

- imagination
- creativity
- stories
- care
- time

A Story World is never finished.

It matures as the child grows.

### Relationship 03 — Belonging

A Storyteller now belongs to VihuPlanet.

They are no longer only visiting.

They now have a place that remembers them.

### Exploration Never Ends

Becoming a Storyteller does not end exploration.

Explorer remains the highest identity inside VihuPlanet.

Storytellers continue exploring the universe.

They may:

- visit Story Worlds
- discover new places
- learn from other Storytellers
- experience new adventures

### Growing the Universe

Storytellers do not simply consume worlds.

They help worlds flourish.

Care, kindness and creativity make the universe richer.

Growth is not limited to a Storyteller's own Story World.

Storytellers may nurture other worlds through love, encouragement and
creativity.

### Product Philosophy

A Storyteller is not defined by an account.

A Storyteller is defined by belonging.

Technology creates an account.

VihuPlanet creates a Storyteller.
