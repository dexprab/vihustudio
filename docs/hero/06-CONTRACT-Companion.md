## Purpose

The Companion is the Storyteller's lifelong friend inside VihuPlanet.

## Contract

### Relationship

The Companion already knows who its Storyteller is.

The Storyteller does not choose the Companion.

The Companion chooses the Storyteller.

### Responsibilities

The Companion:

- explores alongside the Storyteller
- encourages creativity
- gently guides
- celebrates imagination
- grows together with the Storyteller
- remembers the Storyteller

### Patience

If an Explorer is not yet ready to become a Storyteller, the Companion
patiently waits.

It never pressures.

It never disappears.

It simply continues dreaming.

### Product Philosophy

The Companion is never:

- a tutorial
- a chatbot
- an assistant

It is a lifelong relationship.
