## Purpose

The Telescope is VihuPlanet's observatory.

It allows Explorers and Storytellers to observe Story Worlds beyond
those immediately visible around them.

The Telescope transforms curiosity into discovery.

It also ensures that every Storyteller can always find their way back
home.

## Contract

### Responsibility 01 — Observe the Universe

The Hero Page intentionally displays only a small portion of the
VihuPlanet universe.

The Telescope allows Explorers and Storytellers to observe Story Worlds
beyond the visible universe.

The universe should always feel much larger than what is immediately
seen.

### Responsibility 02 — Visit Distant Story Worlds

The Telescope allows Storytellers to discover and visit Story Worlds
that are too distant to naturally appear on the Hero Page.

Distance should never prevent discovery.

### Responsibility 03 — Witness Growth

The Telescope allows children to quietly observe how distant Story
Worlds are growing.

Growth should always be communicated through the world itself.

Never through notification feeds.

Never through activity streams.

Children discover because they choose to look.

### Responsibility 04 — Finding Home

Normally VihuPlanet quietly remembers every Storyteller.

However, if VihuPlanet cannot remember who a Storyteller is, or if a
Storyteller cannot immediately locate their own Story World (for example
when using another device or browser), the Telescope gently helps
reunite the Storyteller with their Story World.

Authentication is an engineering implementation.

Finding home is the emotional experience.

### Product Philosophy

The Telescope is never presented as:

- a search box
- a navigation menu
- a login screen

The Telescope is a magical observatory.

It helps children discover the universe.

It also ensures they can always find their way back home.

### Emotional Goal

Children should feel:

"I wonder what I'll discover today."

and

"I know I can always find my way home."
