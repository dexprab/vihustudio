## Contract

### Relationship Distance

A Storyteller may naturally feel closer to some Storytellers than others.

Story Worlds that become emotionally closer should gradually appear
physically closer inside the Hero Page universe.

Distance reflects relationships.

Distance never reflects popularity.

### Universe Growth

Creativity changes the universe.

Whenever meaningful creativity happens inside a Story World, the Hero
Page should quietly reflect that change.

Examples include:

- gentle glow
- visible growth
- subtle environmental changes

The Hero Page should communicate growth through the world itself rather
than through notifications.

### Product Philosophy

Relationships are expressed spatially.

Creativity is expressed environmentally.

The Hero Page is a living universe rather than a dashboard.
