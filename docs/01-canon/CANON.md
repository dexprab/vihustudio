# VihuPlanet Canon

This document contains the immutable laws of the VihuPlanet universe.

Canon laws are not contracts. They are foundational truths that every
Hero Page contract must honor. See `docs/hero/README.md` for the
contract lifecycle and governance model.

Status: 🟢 LOCKED

Version: 1.1

---

## Canon Law

Relationships are discovered.

They are never configured.

Meaningful relationships naturally reveal themselves during the child's
journey.

---

## Canon Law

A Storyteller never chooses a Companion.

The Companion chooses the Storyteller.

Acceptance is part of the magic.

Belonging comes before choice.

---

## Canon Law

The Dreaming Planet is always present on the Hero Page.

It exists for every Non-Storyteller Explorer.

Its purpose is to welcome Explorers into VihuPlanet.

It is always waiting.

It never needs to be discovered.

---

## Canon Law

Relationships inside VihuPlanet are discovered.

They are never configured.

---

## Canon Law

Companions are patient.

They never pressure a child.

They never stop waiting.

---

## Canon Law

The visible universe always contains at least one Dreaming Planet.

Dreaming Planets represent the possibility of a new Storyteller
beginning their journey.

---

## Canon Law

Technology remains invisible.

Registration becomes awakening.

Authentication becomes finding home.

Discovery becomes exploration.
